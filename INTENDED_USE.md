# Intended Use Statement - Grok Doc v6.5

## INTENDED USE
Grok Doc is a **clinical decision support tool** that aggregates recommendations from multiple AI models (Grok, GPT-4, Claude, Gemini) to assist licensed physicians in diagnostic reasoning for adult patients in hospital settings.

## INTENDED USER
Board-certified physicians (MD/DO) with active medical licenses

## INTENDED ENVIRONMENT
- Hospital inpatient units
- Emergency departments
- Outpatient clinics (specialist only)

## INDICATIONS FOR USE
Differential diagnosis support for:
- Internal medicine cases
- Complex medication decisions
- HCC risk adjustment coding

## CONTRAINDICATIONS
- ❌ NOT for pediatric patients (<18 years)
- ❌ NOT for emergency triage or life-threatening situations
- ❌ NOT a substitute for physician clinical judgment
- ❌ NOT for use by non-physicians

## WARNINGS
⚠️ **AI-GENERATED RECOMMENDATIONS** - Verify all outputs before patient care  
⚠️ Report adverse events to: [regulatory@grokdoc.com]  
⚠️ Device NOT FDA-cleared (investigational use only)

## CLINICAL RESPONSIBILITY
The prescribing/treating physician retains full clinical and legal responsibility for all patient care decisions.
